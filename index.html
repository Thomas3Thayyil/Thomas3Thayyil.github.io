The Great Commission (Matthew 28:19-20)
“Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.”



The love of Jesus is for all. In these difficult times, it is important that we stand by each other and spread the love of Jesus. Send this to 12 people whom you care dearly about, each dedicated to a disciple of our Saviour for a blessed week ahead.
